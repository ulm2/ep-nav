🧩 My City Explorer Game Mode

Turn navigation into a gentle adventure. Play with a parent, helper, teacher, or on your own. Answer simple questions, use visual clues, and unlock jokes + badges.

🏆 Points: 0
Support:
🪴 Adaptive Play Ideas:
Use first-then language, offer two choices at a time, let the child sit while answering, and turn off hover pressure by reading each option slowly. You can also use this indoors with a printed map or picture map.
🧭 Optional Compass Challenge:
Have a trusted adult pick a safe object (tree, bench, sign) and say a bearing like 60°. The child can turn slowly, match the direction, and then read the next clue. Walking is optional.

🎖️ Badge Board

🏞️ Stop 1: Hill, Ridge, or Big Park

Place clue: This could be a hill, ridge, trail area, nature center, or big park in your own city.

🧩 Riddle:

I rise above the desert floor, with rocky trails and views galore. In my city I stand up high, with trails or views beneath the sky. I help explorers look around. What am I?

Answer: A hill, ridge, or big park!

🎁 Reward Joke:

Why do explorers like hills? Because they help you get the big picture! 😄

💧 Stop 2: River, Creek, or Lake

Place clue: Many cities have water nearby, like a river, creek, lake, pond, or canal.

🧩 Riddle:

I may flow, sparkle, or stay still. On maps I help you know where you are. What am I?

Answer: A river, creek, lake, or other water place!

🎁 Reward Joke:

Why did the water clue help the explorer? Because it made the answer flow! 🌊

🏛️ Stop 3: Historic Place or Community Landmark

Place clue: Every city has old buildings, landmarks, memorials, or important gathering places.

🧩 Riddle:

I am old, important, and people remember me. I might be a church, station, memorial, hall, or town landmark. What am I?

Answer: A historic place or community landmark!

🎁 Reward Joke:

Why are old landmarks good listeners? Because they have heard stories for a long, long time! 😄

🧑‍🚒 Stop 4: Community Helper Place

Place clue: Look for a place where helpers work, like a fire station, police station, clinic, post office, or school.

🧩 Riddle:

People come here to help, serve, protect, heal, teach, or deliver. What kind of place might this be?

Answer: A community helper place!

🎁 Reward Joke:

Why do helpers make good teammates? Because you can always count on them! 😊

🎨 Stop 5: Art, Library, or Learning Place

Place clue: This could be a library, museum, art center, nature center, or other learning place in your city.

🧩 Riddle:

Colors splash upon the wall, big and tiny, short and tall. Stories told without a sound, in quiet halls they can be found. What am I?

Answer: An art museum!

🎁 Reward Joke:

Why did the art clue help the explorer? Because it really knew how to draw attention! 🎨
